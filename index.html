About Us

Welcome to Tamshiya

Our Mission

Provides a free, smart travel guide that helps users plan their trips, book activities, and interact with tourist and cultural attractions through a personalized, easy-to-use experience.

Our Goal

Enabling visitors and residents to discover Oman innovatively through advanced technologies such as augmented reality and artificial intelligence contributes to enhancing the tourism sector and supporting the local economy.

Our Vision

The Tamshia app will become the smart companion for every traveler in the Sultanate of Oman. It will provide an integrated tourism experience that combines artificial intelligence and technical interaction to facilitate the fun and smart exploration of local landmarks and activities.

Services

Our Services

Smart Tour Guide

"Beauty of Oman" Discover the rich history of each place you visit without relying on a human guide. Explore monuments, historical sites, and cultural landmarks at your own pace, with detailed information at your fingertips. Immerse yourself in the beauty and history of Oman.

Location and Route Guidance

"Never get lost, even in the most complicated places!" Our smart technology uses interactive arrows and step-by-step directions to guide you effortlessly. Whether you’re in bustling cities or remote regions, our service ensures you never lose your way.

Marketing and Supporting Local Shops

"Discover hidden gems and support local businesses." Unlock special deals from small shops, artisans, and local markets. Whether you’re looking for traditional crafts or modern creations, we help you find unique products while supporting local economy.

Book Special and Unique Experiences

"Make unforgettable memories!" Enjoy unique experiences such as desert safaris, learning to prepare Omani sweets, or guided trekking tours. Our platform offers the chance to experience Oman in a way that few others ever will.

Instant Translation

"The language of the place is no longer a barrier!" With the instant translation feature, tourists can easily understand signs, menus, and communicate with locals. Say goodbye to language barriers and feel at home wherever you are.

Offline use

"Always with you, even without a network!" Save maps and vital information in advance to access them anytime, anywhere. Whether you're in remote locations or simply want to save data, our offline features are there when you need them.

Tourist Challenges and Games

"Make your trip more fun!" Engage in exciting challenges like "Find a hidden landmark" or "Snap a photo of a unique spot." These interactive games will add an element of excitement to your journey, encouraging exploration and discovery!

Event Management

"Plan and manage unforgettable events!" Whether it's a cultural festival, a local market day, or a special tour, our app helps organize and enhance your experience seamlessly. From booking tickets to managing itineraries, we've got you covered.
